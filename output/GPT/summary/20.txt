Phileas Fogg and Aouda, accompanied by Fix, miss their steamer to Yokohama from Hong Kong due to the servant Passepartout's disappearance. Despite this, Fogg remains calm and decides to find another way to reach Yokohama. They explore the docks and negotiate with a sailor, John Bunsby, to take them to Yokohama on his boat. After settling the arrangements, they report Passepartout's disappearance to the police and return to the wharf to set sail.
