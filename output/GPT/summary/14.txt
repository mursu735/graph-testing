Phileas Fogg, Passepartout, and Sir Francis Cromarty rescue Aouda from a human sacrifice ceremony and continue their journey from Kholby to Allahabad by elephant. They reach Allahabad and then take a train to Benares, where Sir Francis departs. Phileas Fogg, Passepartout and Aouda continue on the train to Calcutta, from where they plan to take a steamer to Hong Kong.
