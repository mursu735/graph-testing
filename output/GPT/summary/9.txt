Phileas Fogg, Passepartout, and Detective Fix journey from Suez, Egypt to Bombay, India on the ship "Mongolia". They make a stop at Aden, Yemen to refuel the ship. During the journey, Fogg spends most of his time playing whist, while Passepartout and Fix strike up a friendship. Despite some rough seas, the ship makes good time and arrives in Bombay two days ahead of schedule.
