Phileas Fogg, previously believed to be a criminal, is revealed to be innocent when the real bank robber, James Strand, is arrested in Edinburgh. Fogg's reputation is restored and his journey around the world is once again a topic of public interest. His friends at the Reform Club anxiously await his arrival, speculating whether he will make it in time to win the wager. Despite their doubts, Fogg arrives at the last possible moment, greeted by a crowd of excited spectators.
