The effect of these replies upon the already suspicious and excited detective may be imagined -- Fix's suspicions about Mr. Fogg's true intentions grow stronger.
He reported in a few words the most important parts of his conversation with Passepartout -- Fix informs the consulate about his suspicions regarding Mr. Fogg.
A quarter of an hour later found Fix, with a small bag in his hand, proceeding on board the “Mongolia;” -- Fix boards the ship to follow Mr. Fogg to Bombay.
